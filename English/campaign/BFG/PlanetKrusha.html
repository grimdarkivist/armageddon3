Ork Space Hulk - Planet Krusha

Imperial designation ref: 624456/54 - Dark Harbinger

'I looked upon the vastness of that hulk and knew that we could never win the day. All around, my squadrons had held the line, destroying enemy escorts and capital ships with great Imperial efficiency. But as I watched the stars themselves begin to disappear behind the colossal hull of this evil vessel, my ships began to die. First the Ariadne, then the Proctor Calidor exploded before my eyes as the Dark Harbinger turned its massive long-ranged lance arrays upon them, weapons I had once believed the primitive Orks could never master.

The lesser ships of the greenskin fleet, once cowed by our superlative firepower, surged forward to take advantage of our heavy losses and all the while the big guns of the hulk never let up, bombarding my ships with hideous energies. Bringing His Will forward to counter the threat, the Triumph was left alone too long and I paid for my gamble with the ancient sister ship being crippled in seconds, Captain Honyaeger being forced to disengage. Still that Dark Harbinger moved closer, eager to consume my failing line with an inevitability I had only thought possible from our divine Emperor. As it closed range, I looked upon its unlovely hull, a vast conglomeration of asteroids and ships from fleets that had once belonged to our noble Navy, as well as those of our darkest foes. Pockmarked with damage of battles centuries ago, the skin of the Dark Harbinger hurt my eyes as I gazed with dread upon its surface, the once inspired lines of our own ships now twisted into something much fouler. With a heavy heart, I knew that I could not fight this beast. I signalled the general disengagement.'

- Admiral Parol, after the Defence of Pelucidar
