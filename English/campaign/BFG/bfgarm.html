Greetings commanders of all ranks and all races. Welcome to the Battlefleet Gothic section of the Armageddon campaign. Most of the other information you'll have received by now pertains to the fighting forces and geography of the world of Armageddon. But as any good fleet commander knows, control of a world does not win the ability to exploit it, for that the space-lanes must be cleared and the system dominated by friendly ships. Even if the Imperium can oust the Ork invaders from the surface it will be a hollow victory indeed if the Armageddon system is overrun with Ork pirates, Chaos raiders and Eldar corsairs drawn from within hundreds of light years to feed on the carrion of war. Equally, should Ghazghkull gain control of Armageddon its weapon shops and munitions factories will be useless to him unless their deadly products can be shipped out-system to reinforce the Ork Waaagh sweeping through the Armageddon sector.

So, while the muddy-footed infantry battle it out down on the ground the ultimate test of who controls the future of Armageddon will be resolved in space. You can strike a telling blow with your own fleet and make use of the exciting new Armageddon background for your own battles and campaigns; The space around Armageddon is rife with daring raids, desperate planetary assaults and raging deep space battles between the giant warships of the opposing fleets. So fire up the plasma reactors, clear the gundecks and ready for the battle, the fate of Armageddon rests in your hands.

- Andy Chambers
