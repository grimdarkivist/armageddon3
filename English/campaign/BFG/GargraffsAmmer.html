Ork Kill Kroozer - Gargraff's 'Ammer

The Ork Kroozer Gargraff's 'Ammer (Imperial designation ref: 7742/21 – Icarus' Fate) entered the Third Armageddon War with the first fleet to invade the system, though there is some evidence to suggest that the vessel also took part in the interdictory pirate raids on Minerva. Travelling within the main body of the Ork fleet, the 'Ammer entered combat during the final stages of the Defence of Pelucidar.

Kaptain Gargraff, the ever opportunistic commander of the 'Ammer, saw his chance for glory in Ghazghkull’s eyes as the bulk of the Imperial fleet separated from the battleship Triumph. Ordering his ship forward, the 'Ammer surged towards the lone Triumph with four other Kroozers close behind, intent on breaking the Imperial line to allow the Orks free reign throughout the rest of the Armageddon system. With the Triumph surrounded on all sides, it was only a matter of time before its shields failed completely under the heavy fire of the Ork ships, allowing Gargraff to send boarding parties within assault craft to penetrate the defences of the battleship. As the Triumph began to suffer explosions from within its hull from the boarders moving throughout the ship, Kaptain Gargraff callously ordered the continual bombardment of the stricken vessel, heedless of the lives of the Orks he had only just ordered to board it. Crippled and blazing with fires on multiple decks, the Triumph had little choice but to disengage, opening a hole in the Imperial defence that the Ork ships poured through.

Unlike many of his counterparts on other Ork Kapital ships, Gargraff completely bypassed St. Jowen's Dock to enter the orbit of Armageddon itself with the lead elements of the Ork fleet. These first ships met with a fearsome barrage from Armageddon’s orbital defences and the 'Ammer was amongst the many Ork vessels that fell to the withering fire. Whether it was by luck or design, Gargraff's Meks were able to retain a measure of control over the 'Ammer as the burning ship plummeted through Armageddon's polluted atmosphere. Guiding the Kroozer away from the Tempest Ocean towards the Deadlands, the Meks crash landed the 'Ammer in the wilderness, ploughing a three mile long trench before multiple explosions destroyed what little remained of the ship.

Well aware of the inhuman hardiness of the Ork race, Imperial strategists were little surprised when a reconnaissance flight by the Blue Streaks Thunderbolt squadron revealed Warlord Bogsnik's Blitz Brigade moving away from the wreckage scant hours later.
