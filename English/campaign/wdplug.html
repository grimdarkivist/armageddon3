White Dwarf is the best place to get your monthly fix of Games Workshop excitement. Covering all aspects of the Hobby you simply can't afford to miss an issue!

For the Armageddon campaign in particular, we have a whole host of delights for you:

  • In-depth features on the two great adversaries squaring up to each other in the third war, Ork Warlord Ghazghkull mag uruk Thraka and Imperial Commissar Yarrick.
  • Detailed looks at all of the Chapters of Space Marines taking part in the Armageddon War. Uniform guides, markings, etc... are covered in this extremely useful feature.
  • Inspiring fiction by Dan Abnett. Chronicling the Imperial struggle to regain the burning ruins of Hades Hive from the greenskinned invaders we can guarantee you devastation on a grand scale!
  • A closer look at some of the less well known Imperial Guard regiments such as the Chem-dogs of Savlar and the Armageddon Ork Hunters to name but two.
  • The vast Ork Horde arrayed against the hard-pressed Imperial defenders may seem like an ocean of greenskinned aliens but there are distinctive differences between the tribes forming this invasion. We take a detailed look at a handful of these war-hordes including that of Ghazghkull himself.
  • Index Astartes, our regular look at an aspect of Space Marines delves deeper into the background of the stoic Salamanders chapter. Includes chapter organisation and markings further detailing these warriors of the Promethean Cult.
  • Speed Freeks being one of the army lists introduced in Codex Armageddon it would be remiss of us not to take a look at these velocity-crazed greenskinned maniacs. So we do!
  • Another army highlighted in Codex Armageddon, the Steel Legion has along and bloody history. This article chronicles some of the great battles they have fought in.
  • In a not-altogether-serious feature we once again enter the Arena of death and pit two special characters against each other! The mighty Ghazghkull takes on his nemesis Commissar Yarrick in a conflict that doesn't quite end how we planned...
  • An undertaking of this magnitude would be incomplete without a battle report and we've a plan for something rather special - a battle between eleven players, over four battlefields and spanning two separate issues of White Dwarf! This is undoubtedly the biggest and most complex battle report to ever have been recorded so don't say we didn't warn you!

  • In another Index Astartes, the Black Templars chapter comes under the scrutiny of our humble scribes. Featuring a detailed background to this most fervent of Space Marine chapters and details of one crusades organisation along with a beautifully painted example of a Black Templars army this perfectly complements the information found in Codex Armageddon.
  • At the end of the campaign we'll collate all the information from all the games the world over and when the dust finally settles we'll be able to tell who has emerged victorious. We'll detail lots of game reports, heroic actions and remarkable events and round things off nicely with a... well, you'll have to wait to find out about that won't you!
  • Phew! And that's not all - the list above only covers the support we are giving to Codex Armageddon and the global campaign. Each issue will also bring you great articles for our other games, specifically our game of fantasy battles, Warhammer.
  • Add to this regular free gifts such as free posters, codex books and even miniatures and I'm sure you 'll agree that you can't get by in the Games workshop hobby without White Dwarf magazine so what are you waiting for - pick up the latest edition or even better take a look at the excellent subscription offers we have for you.
