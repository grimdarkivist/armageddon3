Ork Forces

Warlord Morfang

Warlord Morfang peered out of the bridge vision slits of his great gargant, the Gut Rolla. He had long since been used to the constant rolling gait of the huge war machine and held on to the rusted metal rails along the wall as he gazed upon his massed army. Warlords Skarfang and Burzuruk had added their warriors to his own for the coming battle and the sight of so many Orks eager for battle did, in truth, move Morfang.

Bellowing instructions above the deafening noise of the gargant's boilers to rotate the machine's massive head, hew saw the other six gargants of his personal Big Mob following him. Their clumsy movements matched those of the Gut Rolla as one of them, the 'Ead Kicka, Morfang thought, raised the huge mega kannon in its right arm towards the sky, letting loose a giant shell as its crew became ever more excited over the impending battle. Leading the gargants' march were numerous stompas, tiny parodies of the much larger machines, but they still dwarfed the Orks warriors around them. As far as Morfang could see, mobs of his combined army covered the ash wastes, their guttural cries and challenges reaching the Warlord's ears, even over the cacophony of the Gut Rolla. Thousands of Orks were piled into hundreds of trukks and buggies, but many more ran alongside the war machines, ever wary of being accidentally trodden upon. Morfang did not know how many Ork warriors were under his command exactly, but he knew they would be unbeatable in battle.

A grot tugging at his boot got his attention, the little greenskin informing the Warlord that the human army had been sighted by the observation post on the shoulder of his gargant. Kicking the unfortunate creature back to its station, he ordered the Gut Rolla's head to be turned back to face the enemy. Morfang grinned as he watched the assembled Imperial army. A handful of titans were moving inexorably towards him, the tanks and infantry of the humans milling about at their feet. Morfang's smile slipped a little as he made a quick estimation of the humans' strength.

'Ladz. . .' he said to the rest of the Orks on the gargant's bridge. 'I hope there iz enough for all of us!'
