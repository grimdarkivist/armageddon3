"Armageddon, a world whose name is known across the Imperium. Armageddon, a world whose name has become a byword for war and destruction on a massive scale. Armageddon, where the fate of a thousand worlds hangs in the balance."

- Lord Commander Solar Eugenian

Strategically placed within the Segmentum Solar, the Armageddon sub-sector and in particular the hive world Armageddon is vital to the maintenance of the Imperium of mankind's power in this area. It is unsurprising therefore that it should have such a bloodstained past...

Under the baleful gaze of the Daemon Primarch Angron, favoured of Khorne the Blood God, a vast Chaos army fell upon the loyal forces of Armageddon. Despite armed rebellion inexplicably breaking out across half a dozen hive cities the insidious tendrils of treachery were gradually eradicated in the Armageddon Secundus region but the more scattered hives of Armageddon Primus meant that the rebel forces were more difficult to contain...

When the Ork Warlord Ghazghkull mag uruk Thraka spearheaded the invasion of Armageddon at the head of an enormous Ork Waagh the planet was under the command of Overlord Herman von Strab, a bureaucrat with no discernible command qualities. It is unsurprising then that von Strab was completely unprepared for the Ork onslaught despite several clear warning signs. Von Strab's pride compounded his incompetence and the Orks made massive inroads into Imperial territory. It fell to an Imperial Commissar by the name of Yarrick to mobilise the defence of the planet and to try and stem the flow of greenskins pouring across the planet's surface like a savage green tide...

Although believed dead at the culmination of the second Armageddon war, it soon became clear that Ghazghkull's demise was exaggerated and that in the fifty years since the end of the second war he had not been idle. What follows on this website are the events leading to the greatest Ork invasion force seen since the days of the great Solar Macharius crashing down upon the Armageddon sub-sector...
