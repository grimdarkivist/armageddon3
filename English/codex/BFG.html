Battlefleet Gothic, the game of spaceship combat, is set during the Gothic War in the 41st Millennium; a grim time when the many races of the far future battle for survival in a hostile galaxy. Battelfleet Gothic allows you to command fleets of warships in deadly conflict among the stars!

Make sure you grab this opportunity to battle in the cold void of space above the war-torn planet of Armageddon. Who will control this strife-ridden sector? Only you can decide!

The Battlefleet Gothic boxed game contains: the Battlefleet Gothic rulebook with campaign rules, 4 Imperial cruisers (multi-part kits), 4 Chaos cruisers (multi-part kits), 12 assorted dice, Fleet Registry roster pad, 2 plastic range rulers, over 100 game counters, 2 reference sheets and a "Getting Started" guide with an exclusive comic strip.
