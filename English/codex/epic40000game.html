Epic 40,000 allows you to take your battles in the 41st Millennium to a whole new scale! Now, you can be the Commander of a Space Marine Chapter, or a fierce Ork Warlord leading a mighty Waaagh! Epic 40,000 gives you the ability to wage war across entire battle fronts and planets, do you have what it takes to coordinate the defense of Armageddon or will you perish under the sheer weight of the Ork onslaught?

With Epic 40,000 you can fight battles across the tabletop using vast armies of Citadel Miniatures that you assemble, paint, and collect yourself to represent your chosen force. Build a huge Ork WAAAGH! of foot troops and tanks complete with a horde of crazed Speed Freeks! The armies you create can become truly massive forces of war! What's really cool though is the ability to customize the detachments of your battlegroup to match the armies you use in Warhammer 40,000, practically squad for squad!

The Epic 40,000 boxed set contains everything you need to get started right away. Along with all the rulebooks, dice, counters, and templates, you get the core forces of two very different armies, the Space Marines and the vicious Orks. The Space Marines are the finest warriors of the Imperium and humanity's greatest defenders. On the other hand, the Orks crave battle above everything else, and when they begin to gather nothing can withstand their might for long. You'll get loads of plastic Ork Battlewagons, Stompas, Boyz and specialist squads like Stormboyz as well as Imperial Land Raider tanks, Whirlwinds, Rhinos, and Terminators!

While Space Marines and Orks come in the Epic 40,000 boxed set, they are but two of the numerous forces of the 41st Millennium. Full rules for Eldar, Chaos, Tyranids, and Imperial Guard are also included in the rulebook and army books in the main game.
