Warhammer 40,000 is a tabletop wargame for two or more players where you command the forces of the Imperium or one of its deadly enemies. The game provides you with all the rules, scenarios and army lists you need to fight a battle, and guides you through the process of painting the detailed plastic Citadel Miniatures in the box!

When you buy this boxed set, you have equipped yourself with all the tools you'll need to start playing in the amazingly detailed Third War for Armageddon. Make sure to get in on the action now and decide the fate of the planet!

The Warhammer 40,000 boxed game contains: 288 page rulebook, 10 plastic Space Marines, 1 plastic Land Speeder, 20 plastic Dark Eldar Warriors, 7 assorted game dice, 2 plastic range rulers, 3 plastic weapon templates, 1 set of gothic ruins, 4 complete jungle trees, assorted barricades and 2 card playsheets!
