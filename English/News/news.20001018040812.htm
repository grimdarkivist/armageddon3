Welcome to Armageddon!

August 4, 2000 - Welcome to the Third War for Armageddon Campaign. From here you will be able check out the status of the war across all of the different Warzones of this battle-scarred planet, the latest news, overall statistics and more. Come back often to check up on the status of your Warzones and to see how your contribution to the conflict is affecting the overall battle. Thanks for playing and remember, the fate of Armageddon is in your hands!
