Welcome to the War in Space!

August 3, 2000 - Welcome to the Armageddon Sector - the Battlefleet Gothic Warzone of the Third War for Armageddon Campaign. From here you will be able check out the status of the war across all of the different regions of the Armageddon Sector, overall statistics and more. Come back often to check up on the status of your subsectors and to see how your contribution to the conflict is affecting the overall battle.
