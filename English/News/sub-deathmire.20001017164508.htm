The War So Far...

August 9, 2000 - The subsectors of Death Mire are seeing some of the most vicious combat yet witnessed in the Armageddon War. While it seems that the flood of Feral Orks pouring from the Equatorial Jungle has been stemmed for the time being, Ork troops have devastated the Mire Anchorage and it looks like there is little hope of it being returned to Imperial hands any time in the near future.

The defenses of the Tempestor Victorum Line are holding fast and the Greenskins can do little to stop the pounding they are taking from Imperial gunnery crews stationed there. The capture of Hellstrom Fortress by Ghazghkull's troops is disturbing as the siege guns and mine casters emplaced there could do tremendous damage to Imperial forces if Ork mechanics can figure out how to use them properly.
