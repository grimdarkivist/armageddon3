"Today we can but begin what must surely be a long and arduous task - to assess and describe the Steel Daemons, to confront them and ultimately to bring them to battle. We can but add guesses to our too scant knowledge, to reconstruct our weaponry, their fighting methods, their strengths and vulnerabilities. All this we must do, and quickly, lest we perish without ever recognising our peril."

- Inquisitor Hoth

Although there are few verifiable facts about the mechanical horrors which are known to the learned as the Necrons, we can summarize what little we do know. The Necrons are a biologically dead race which was wiped out in an ancient galactic catastrophe. Faced with extinction, the Necrons constructed metal bodies as repositories for their hyper-advanced minds. To ensure that they would continue to exist after the impending galactic bio-meltdown, the Necrons constructed huge stasis chambers to shelter inside.

They have been there ever since, resting until such a time that the galaxy was safe for them to emerge. The discovery of some of these stasis chambers has triggered the emergence of isolated groups of Necrons all over the galaxy. Quite what their purpose is remains a mystery. Necrons have so far confined their activities to vicious raids. They are known to perform grisly biological experiments upon human prisoners.

Some believe that the Necrons, possessed as they are of a vastly superior knowledge, are attempting to rebuild their race in biological form. Others hypothesize that the Necrons are no longer possessed of rational minds. Whatever the reason for their attacks, the only thing that is know for sure is that if the Necrons suddenly appear on the battlefield - be afraid. Be very afraid!
