Among the Eldar are those who are bloodthirsty and war-hungry in the extreme. None can say where they came from, but they strike without warning in the depths of space or suddenly appear in orbit of a planet. They are utterly ruthless, and appear to take a positive delight in the infliction of pain and misery. Those not killed by their horrendous weapons are made captive and are taken back to whatever wretched den of iniquity serves these pirates as home.

Dark Eldar troops and vehicles emphasise speed and firepower at the expense of heavy armour, and so they avoid long firefights and frontal assaults. Instead they scythe round the flanks and rear of the enemy, using thier fast Raider transports to make slashing hit and run attacks and cut the enemy's strength down ready for the final killing blow. They use the same tactics on the defensive, harrying the attacker from cover, falling back when the enemy get too close, then launching a vicious counter attack once the attackers have been bled white.
