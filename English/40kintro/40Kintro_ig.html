"Imperial Guardsmen, you are the last line of defence against the alien hordes that threaten our Imperium. Each one of you brave warriors is a part of a vast fighting force that will be called upon to serve in the eternal war. you will fight on countless battlefields on a thousand different worlds across the galaxy.

And, whether you fight in our great crusading armies or serve with the smallest garrison, you must be proud to sacrifice your life to save Humanity from its enemies."

- Commisar Yarrick, addressing new recruits

The innumerable warriors of the Imperial Guard form the backbone of the Imperium's fighting strength. Their numbers are so vast and their campaigns so widely scattered across the galaxy that even the Adepts of Earth have little idea of their exact strength and deployment. After a conflict has ended, the regiments involved are often left where they are, forgotten until years, or even decades, later.

While individual Guardsmen cannot match the strength, ferocity and ruthlessness of the Space Marines, Imperial Guard regiments are powerful fighting forces comprising thousands of men with hundreds of tanks, artillery pieces and armoured vehicles. Even the smallest detachment of Imperial Guardsmen is likely to be supported by a Leman Russ battle tank or Chimera fighting vehicles. In larger battles the Imperial Guard can deploy whole phalanxes of tanks and vehicles backed up by swarms of infantry and long-range artillery.
