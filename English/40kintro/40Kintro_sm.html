"The enemies of the Emperor fear many things,
They fear discovery, defeat, despair and death.
Yet there is one thing they fear above all others
They fear the wrath of the Space Marines."

The Space Marines are the most feared and respected of all of mankind's warriors. Standing over seven feet tall, genetically engineered then extensively trained they are the ultimate human fighting machine. Formed into a thousand chapters, each a thousand Space Marines strong. Their prowess and bravery means that they can defeat opponents who outnumber them many time over.

Equipped with the best arms and armour the Imperium can offer, Space Marine armies are also extremely mobile, able to move swiftly to deal with an enemy threat or top strike suddenly to seize a vital strategic objective. The Space Marines' dedication and fearless devotion to their chapter make them ideal shock troops, their power armour and excellent training allowing them to take on seemingly impossible odds and emerge victorious!

Although most Space Marine chapters adhere closely to the Codex Astartes, the texts dictating the organisation and conduct of a Space Marine chapter, not all follow its strict dictates. Amongst these are the Blood Angels, bloodthirsty warriors excelling in close combat; the Black Templars, zealous crusaders; the Dark Angels, steadfast warriors harbouring a dark secret; the Space Wolves, ferocious, headstrong and deadly Space Marines.
