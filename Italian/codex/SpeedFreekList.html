Orks just love to zip around in fast vehicles like Buggies and Warbikes. However, some Orks become addicted to the exhilaration and thrill of going fast. These Orks are known as Speed Freeks. Ork Speed Freeks love to drive around so much, that they rarely leave their vehicles for anything, except to customize them! These Ork nutters have congregated with other such afflicted Orks in the ash wastes of Armageddon to form up masses of lightly armored vehicles that hit the weary Imperial defenders without warning!

Ork players looking for a change of pace and tactics will love the Speed Freeks. The army utilizes blinding speed to create surprise hit and run attacks. It also enables you to collect and model a force composed entirely of mechanized mayhem! Every Speed Freek vehicle requires a team of Grot Riggers that constantly maintain the vehicle's integrity. Sometimes the little buggers even manage to fix major problems! Having these models all over your Buggy adds loads of character to the standard vehicle.

Hurtle into battle with a massive amount of hard hitting Warbikes and Trukks full of deadly Slugga Boyz! Take to the skies and terrorize dug in enemy units with awesome units of Deth Koptas! The Speek Freek army is the ultimate chance to test your tactical skill with a veritable legion of speed crazed Orks. As long as you stick to the age old Orky adage "Red Ones Go Fasta", you can't go wrong!
