Coinciding with this gargantuan gaming extravaganza we're also releasing Codex Armageddon. This new codex focuses on the return of Waaagh! Ghazghkull to the strategically vital Armageddon sub-sector and in particular the colossal war threatening consume the entire planet. Written by Andy Chambers, Jervis Johnson and Gavin Thorpe this new codex is broken down into three distinct sections:

This section outlines the dark history of the war-shrouded world, a world where war is a way of life. Descriptions of the forces arrayed on both sides of the battleline.

Detailed chronologies of the events leading to all out war and the past conflicts that have promised to cripple this important Imperial world can also be found within this books pages and also chronicles biographies of the main protagonists in the 3rd war for Armageddon. This section also explains how to get the most from your games set within the 3rd war.

A section of full colour pages showcasing superbly painted miniatures representing those taking part in this bloody war. There are uniform guides for the multitudinous Imperial Guard regiments and Space Marine Chapters defending Armageddon, as well as a look at the tribal markings of the more distinctive Ork warbands that Ghazghkull Thraka has formed into vast greenskinned throng poised to sweep the planet into oblivion.

Codex Armageddon also contains four brand new army lists:

  • Ork Speed Freeks
    - Vehicle mad and speed-addicted greenskins who use their mobility to get 'up close and personal!
  • The Armageddon Steel Legions
    - A mechanised Imperial Guard infantry regiment.
  • The Salamanders Chapter of Space Marines
    - Pragmatic, unyielding battle brethren.
  • The Black Templars Chapter of the Adeptus Astartes
    - Fanatical, crusading Space Marines.
