ABOUT HELS HIGHWAY

Hels Highway is the lifeline that connects the vital water and oil supply depots of Hive Helsreach to all of Armageddon Secundus. This motorway is vastly important and its appearance is quite deceiving. Below the surface of Hels Highway is a mass of underground pipelines that bring water and oil to other Hives and settlements of the interior.

This underground supply system also features a classified high speed railroad known as the Dart. This mag-lev track transport is used by high-ranking Imperial officials to move safely between the Hives during peacetime. When the Third War for Armageddon began, the Dart was immediately requisitioned for military use. Although too small to move vast numbers of troops, it has been used to transfer small units of engineers, commandos and hunter-killer teams to help stave off the Ork invasion where the fighting is most desperate.

The area of Hive Helsreach which opens up onto Hels Highway is brimming with industrial equipment, pumping stations, toxic sludge ponds, refineries, gigantic storage tanks and Administratum offices which monitor the coming and going of supplies above and (secretly) below the road. The highway entrances to the city proper are heavily mined and defended.

Massive bombardments have opened up holes in the surface of Hels Highway and the Orks have discovered the underground pipeline system. The battle is now waged above and below the white line nightmare that is this vital supply route.

Below the surface of Hels Highway, the labyrinth of pipes, corridors and tunnels is known as the Maze by the underhive work details which labor amid the darkness. While Ork Kommandos prowl the Maze in an attempt to map out a route into Hive Helsreach itself , Hive Militia and Imperial defenders hunt greenskins in an effort to exterminate them before they find their way into the city. If a path can be found, Helsreach is doomed!
