As the battle across the Plains of Showcayz begann, we knew that this would be our last push to retake the Ministorum temple which had been engulfed by the Greenskin horde. Driving on the dug in Orks were a dozen Armored Fist Squads, and a full division of Leman Russ and Basilisks. By midday these mighty engines of the Emperor's wrath (blessed be his name) had recovered about half the field, but Ork reinforcements still held the temple and we knew that the Greenskins were nothing if not cunning.

It was Colonel Randard of the 32nd Jopall Indentured who conceived of a plan bold enough to commit our forces before the Orks desecrated the Temple beyond repair and dealt a vicious blow to Imperial morale. However, whether his tactics would result in a successful mission was a matter of debate among the command staff. Under cover of darkness, a squad of Imperial Storm Troopers had infiltrated behind the Ork lines and taken up a position near the Imperial temple. When the Greenskin sentries were disposed of and a direct path to the objective was clear, they activated the Teleport Homer which summoned a Space Marine Strike Team from an orbiting Strike Cruiser. Though they died to the last man trying to protect it, their valiant actions allowed four squads of Salamanders, including Chaplain Xavier himself, to teleport in.

Realizing that their prize was about to fall, the Orks sent their mobile units racing to the chapel while their boyz mobs held Imperial assault teams on the front lines back. The Salamanders pushed their way to the Chapel again and again, truly living up to their tenacious and methodical reputation. Each time the Orks pushed them back with a ferocity which matched the Salamanders' determination. Ork Choppa and Space Marine combat blade chopped through the ranks with equal fury as precious Space Marine vitae and corrupted Ork ichor fell in vast amounts in the trenches!

Finally, as night was falling Xavier entered the Chapel and retrieved the Holy Relics within including a precious vial purpoted to contain the tears of the Emperor himself. Though this has done wonders for the morale of our Imperial forces which are now pinned down in the area outside of the temple complex, the Orks still hold this strategic building and have easy access to Hive Helsreach's southern flank.

Due to his obvious incompetence under fire, Colonel Randard has been summarily executed on order of General Milnivil, who was then shot by his own Commissar. Case file on these incidents will be closed pending review by the Inquisition.
