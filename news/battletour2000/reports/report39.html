"Orks lack common sense, and that's a good thing!"

After a week of setting our trap the Orks finally rose to the bate and bit, praise the Emperor. We had two platoons of Valhallans guarding the road up from the sea, while a small force of Sisters guarded the church. The High Command's plan was simple: let the orks think their way into Helsreach from the South was only lightly defended. Once the green skinned scum charged our positions, reinforcements would arrive and smash them from both sides closing the jaws of the trap. Despite heavy losses, the morning engagement went as planned. The Orks drove forward to our positions and sent a small force towards the Temple on our left flank. We hit them with everything we had and were able to destroy the forces sent against the temple. The center did not fair so well. The loss of Colonel Ormond and Commissar Norquest was a heavy blow to Imperial moral and the Orks occupied all of the ground that the Valhallans had held earlier in the day. Then the real counter attack began.

Eight Chimeras of the Steel Legion sweeped in from the right while more Valhallans and Sisters poured froth from the cathedral to take up prepared positions on the left. The green skins put up a fight, stalling the Steel Legion advance, but taking heavy losses in return. On the right the orcs advanced towards the cathedral, but many of their mobile units were destroyed. As night neared the Imperial command bunker, now in ork possession was only lightly defended. Seeing his advantage General Dittman III sent in his reserves of Salamanders and a flight of Seraphim, which took back the Imperial command bunker. With the bunker again in our possession, the automated defense bunkers could be brought on line and short work was make of the orcs. The few orc survivors fled under the cover of darkness.
