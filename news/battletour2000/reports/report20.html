The left flank near the Cathedral was facing a horde of grots at the start of the battle. Within minutes, the little annoyances turned upon their fellow greenskins and this helped turn the tide at the steps of the Cathedral. Our lines wavered in the varied support of the little ones and each man's faith in the Emperor. After one of the Sergeants was summarily executed over vid link, the cheering and support of the Grots ceased. I do not relish the killing of our men, but an example had to be made. The men began killing greenskins regardless of size and once again found their faith in the Emperor a powerful tool to give them the strength to carry on the fight for Armageddon.
