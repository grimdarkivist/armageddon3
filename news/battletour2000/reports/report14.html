"Return to the light of the Emperor or die."

The feelings of what has happened to our commander are split between the troops. If the need for manpower was not so great there is no doubt that we would not be alive this day. I have been given a field commission over the Steel Legion forces in this sector. The revolution of those little pests continues but after what has happened to the past commander I am unsure as to what action to take, if any. I have told my men to fire on them if they see them as a threat and that situation has arose a few times. If they in turn try to fire upon us they will feel the full might of the Emperor's armies but for now I shall let them die for our cause.

To top off the split feelings of my troops, some of them are falling to the rehertic of that damned Von Strab. In fact, there is word that he would send troops to show his support of the Orks in the upcoming battles, only time will tell...

The Orks took the field and tried to overwhelm the bastion of hope that is the Cathedral. It has stood for all of the wars of Armageddon and has proved to be a valuable part of morale for our forces. I have received reports of some humans fighting alongside of the Greenskins, they may be Von Strab's men but there is nothing that can confirm this. The Orks made countless advances on the Cathedral but were stopped at every turn. The highlight of the day came when two squads of Adeptus Arbites emerged from the chapel to shoot and then wipe out a squad of Mega Armored Orks in hand to hand. This is just one of the many unsung acts of valor that have taken part on this field.

One of the mortar shells deviated and hit the Grots that were engaged in hand to hand with a mob of Orks. The corporal said it was not intentional, but intentional or not the little traitors opened up on everyone, human and Ork. Then they vanished into the ruins of the city only to show up assaulting one of my Basilisks. The Salamanders came in at the end of the battle to dispatch the humans that were allying with the Orks, the rumor still stands that the Terminator sergeant dispatched Von Straub himself but this report cannot be verified.
