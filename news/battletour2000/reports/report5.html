Radar scans from the night before detected a small group of Ork Fighta Bommaz flying low over Imperial Core IT82. This single core station supplies power for several defensive measures along Hel's Highway. These measures include Anti Aircraft batteries and several automated defensive units like those at outposts J-72 and K-76. Naturally these Ork planes arose a bit of suspicion and measures were taken against them. A few well placed shots from a Steel Legion Platoon brought these Re-con planes down.

Two of the planes were enveloped by their own flames and destroyed in huge exlosions before making contact with the ground. The one remaining plane crashed a few miles East of Imperial Core IT82. Units were mobilized to find the recon unit and make sure any data it may have picked up was destroyed. Imperial Core IT82 is an integral part of the Hel's Highway defense system, and it's low key nature is it's best defense. Should the Orks learn it's true purpose, their terrible crusade will be made ten times easier.

Upon arrival, at the spproximate crash site, Orks were detected in the area. Apparently the Fighta Bommaz had been sent with purpose. Curse the foul vermin! Despite their feral nature, we must not underestimate their intelligence.

Imperial Guard Steel Legion units were ordered to silence the Speed Freeks to the east of the downed Fighta Bomma, while the Salamanders were given the task of cleansing the western front of a large mass of Orks on foot.

As the Steel Legion neared their targets, another wave of Deth Kopters sped into the fray completely overrunning the eastern flank. The victorious Speed Freeks sped towards the objective unhindered. All seemed lost, but the Salamanders bravely held their own against the alien horde, buying time as reinforcements began to filter in from Imperial Core IT82. Although the Orks had indeed retrieved the recon data from the wreckage, by the Emperor they would not escape with it.

Heavy support in the form of Leman Russ amd Basilisk tanks rumbled into the battlezone, pounding the Orks as they attemped to leave with the data. Another mechanized legion of Chimera carriers cut off their retreat and held them up. The cost of human life was great, but their blood did not go to waste. The Salamanders pursuit finished off the Ork scum and the data was once again in Imperial hands. It appears the Imperium had barely escaped a serious loss to the defensive of Hel's Highway, we must be more careful.
