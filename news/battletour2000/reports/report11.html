"Bullets we need more bullets!"

We took our positions among the ruins of our once great hive. The majority of the outlying spires had been decimated. The "Cathedral of Everlasting Hope" was one of the last bastions of the Emperor's light and we could not let it fall to the tide of Orks that have overwhelmed Armageddon. The Asgardian Rangers have been reassigned from the Deadlands and have come to support my Steel Legion squads. My men have not slept in days but we cannot let the weakness show. The forces that stand around us have come to save our planet so we must set the example and be ever vigilant.

The fighting began with promise with our tanks delivering massive destruction to the initial Ork attack. The tide began to turn when the Orks did not stop their advance. The red wheels of their vehicles turning up the dust of what was once our home. The Orks halted the attack to an extent and appeared to be searching through a downed titan from the Legio Ignatum. Our main bunker that held the mortar positions was overwhelmed with the smaller but no less vicious Greenskins.

The situation appeared hopeless as my men dropped like razorwings in the Fire Wastes. The comm-links blared the arrival of the Salamanders. Their green armor deflected shot after shot from the massive horde but their presence was not enough. The Orks were running over our positions except for the Cathedral. This would be our last stand. I ordered the remnants of my platoon to join the Rangers in the Cathedral and hold out as long as possible. I must find a way to get reinforcements, any reinforcements. Only time will tell...

Captain Projan
