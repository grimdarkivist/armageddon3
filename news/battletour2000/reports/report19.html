The orders came in this morning that we must advance and take back the ground that was lost in the previous weeks. This was not going to be an easy task and it would prove to be a challenge with the resources at hand. We knew that the Orks were running low on supplies and could not hold out for too much longer. To stop the sympathy for the grotz that had been running rampant through Imperial lines, the Basilisk crews were instructed to blow them off the planet no matter what target presented itself.

-Captain Ty: XXX Armageddon Steel Legion

The Orks managed to steal some fuel from one of the Hellhounds on the left flank. This enabled them to bring on some reinforcements in the form of their bikes. Wave after wave of Greenskins came upon us to assault the last stronghold left, the ammo bunker. Even the Salamander Space Marines were no match for the Orks as they overwhelmed our forces and made off with the ammo. We will regroup and seek out the Ork threat on its own ground.
