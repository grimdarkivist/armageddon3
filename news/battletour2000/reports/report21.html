The Orks were hiding amongst the ruins outside the hive and were awaiting the fall of darkness before they attacked. We took the time to ensure that all the spotlights on our vehicles were in working order as we would be depending on them in the failing light. We worked on setting up barricades and kill zones. We would not let any Orks pass this night, lest we fail the populace of Hive Helsreach.

We set up our battle command post in a large Imperial building where we would have good lines of sight and fields of fire. We could see some Orks off in the distance silhoutted by some of the burning ruins but they were too far away to shoot at. We could tell they were getting ready to mount another attack and we were calling for more tanks but the request was denied.

We heard the starting of numerous engines and the time for battle was near. The deafening roar of all those engines made our heads swim but we held fast and prepared our weapons and our faith. We could hear the bikes zipping around and we could catch glimpses of them. We opened fire with everything we had, and our artillery smashed into their units in a walking barrage. But still they came at us.

Our position in the building gave a wide view of the battle, everywhere there was shouts and explosions. We could see the Ork Koptas zoom up on the left flank but the amount of fire they received more than hurt them. Koptas by the dozens dropped from the sky and exploded amongst the Ork units below.

On the right, several tanks moved onto the motorway to block the Ork bikers from getting past. Their guns fired as fast as they could and caused a heavy toll on the bikers. Some of the bikers were able to get past and they fired their guns and lobbed bombs at the tanks, but they were quickly picked off by the Ratling Snipers hiding in a few of the buildings beyond the tanks. No Ork made it past the line of tanks on the right flank. The sheer volume of firepower was too much for the Orks and they were slowly pushed back.

Ork Stormboyz jumped up to our building and tossed in some grenades to try to get us to run out. We threw grenades back at them and the Stormboyz burst in and a hard fought combat ensued. The Stormboyz were starting to get the best of us and more began pouring through the door. An Ork kill team jumped into the room and began to flame every Ork in the room as well as the ones pouring through the door. The Orks howled in pain and we countinued to hack at the Orks that we still in melee. We outnumbered them and the smarter Orks decided to get out of the building while they could and ran.
