The orders arrived today that we must begin our push to the sea. The Steel Legion will lead the way and will be the moral booster that the troops need so desperately. The initial rush took some casualities but there were light considering. The Grotz are playing a major role in this battle and have already destroyed three squads of the greenskins along with a squad of Mega Armored Orks. I am glad I have associated my forces with them and the reinforcements I have asked for have finally arrived. My flanking force reports that the Dark Angels have arrived led by the Ravenwing. Now the tide shall truly turn in our favor.

The battle is reaching its climax, this will be my last entry until the post battle report to HQ.
