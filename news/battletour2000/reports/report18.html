With a renewed sense of purpose the Imperial Guard have rallied around Commissar Yarrick. Using stout silence in the face of the boistrous Ork horde, the forces of the Imperium have gained the momentum that is needed to carry them on to victory. The only answer the Orks will have is that of their bodies being crushed by the tracks of our Chimeras.

Our goal was to get as many of the Steel Legion forces of Hel's highway leading to the dock. This came to pass because of a Basilisk shot that destroyed one of the suicide trukks that was heading toward our lines. The ensuing explosion destroyed a quarter of the Ork forces that entrenched themselves in the way of our objective. Our forces were not exempt of the mayhem caused by the explosion. The lone Salamander that was defending his fallen Battle Brothers joined them. Two Chimeras along with two of Yarricks bodyguards were destroyed and even Commissar Yarrick himself was thrown to the ground. This did not set Yarrick's back and when he rose from the crater he was swamped by the Ork Warboss and his retunue of Nobz.

In a spectacular display of courage and skill, Yarrick managed to dispatch the Nobz and the Warboss. With a man like him leading the forces of Armageddon, we may yet have a chance.

-Commissar Timma
