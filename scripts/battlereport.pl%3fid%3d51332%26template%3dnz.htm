Battle Description:
Interesting battle. The imperium could have one but those silly black templars weren't expecting a dreadnought and didn't have much to stop it. If they had commited all their forces to killing it straight away perhaps they might have one. Grimzag fell once again under a hail of marines but not before taking a lot with him. The orks sqaud charged up under constant hail of whirlwind fire with the lootas unable to hit anything. Once in combat though the marines knew they had lost as they tried to destroy the dreadnought but could not as there hk missile flew wide. The dreadnought laughed as he destroyed the last things that could hurt it and ran round picking up the pieces. Last thing standing: One large ork dreadnought, unblemished.
